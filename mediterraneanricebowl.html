Mediterranean Rice Bowl

Description

This is an easy dish that can be made in approximately 50 minutes.

The ingredients are simple and you can substitute for any vegetables you'd like!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of basmati or jamsmine rice
  • 5 tbs olive oil
  • 1 med-large lemon
  • Olives (any kind)
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • 1/2 package cherry tomatoes
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 package green beans
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps

Rice + Base

  1. Cook 1 and 1/2 cups of rice in rice cooker
  2. Cut up cucumber, tomatoes, olives, and avocado
  3. Cook green beans and cut them into ~1.5 inch pieces
  4. Let rice cool down to room temperature in the refrigerator and combine with vegetables.

Dressing

  1. Put olive oil + garlic in a small bowl
  2. Squeeze the juice out of the lemon into the small bowl
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Combine rice/vegetable base with dressing and mix in large bowl.
  5. Serve at room temperature or cold.
