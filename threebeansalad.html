Three Bean Salad

Description

This is a delicious and easy dish that can be used as a side for any dinner.

Or, if you just need a snack, this can be an incredibly healthy option! It's easy to put together and is very cost effective.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. can of green beans
  • 15 oz. can of white beans
  • 15 oz. can of red kidney beans
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)

Steps

Bean Base

  1. Add rinsed beans to a large bowl
  2. Finely chop 1 cup of parsley and add to bowl
  3. Cut celery into small pieces and add to bowl
  4. Cut onion into fine pieces and add to bowl (being careful not to cry)

Dressing

  1. Combine apple cider vinegar and olive oil
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste
  3. Add sugar, dijon mustard, and salt/pepper, if being used
  4. Combine bean base with dressing and put in the refrigerator to chill overnight
  5. Let come to room temperature before serving
