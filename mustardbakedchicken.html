Mustard Baked Chicken

Description

This is an easy dish that can be made in under one hour.

The ingredients are simple and the recipe is easy to follow.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole chicken breasts
  • 4 tbs dijon mustard (whole grain)
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into 1/2 inch thick slices
  2. Mix with mustard and let marinate for 30-60 minutes
  3. Place on baking sheet and cook until temperature reaches 155 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Remove from oven and let sit for 3-5 minutes prior to serving.
